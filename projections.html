How the Math Works (Transparency First)

Let P = annual payout per participating person (the 80% share).

Total paid by buyers (100%) = P / 0.80 = 1.25P
Infrastructure (15% of total) = 0.15 × 1.25P = 0.1875P
Treasury (5% of total) = 0.05 × 1.25P = 0.0625P

What This Means for LivePay

Your split produces three predictable national "budgets" that scale with adoption and payout levels:

1. People's Dividend Stream (80%)
The big one that drives legitimacy and virality. At full participation with moderate payouts ($3,500/year), this puts $1.20 trillion directly into the hands of Americans annually—money they earned by creating the data that powers the AI economy.
2. Infrastructure Budget (15%)
Funds the "centralized mesh broker" rails: audits, compliance, attribution, secure payouts, and the technical backbone that makes fair data markets possible. At scale, this generates $224.3 billion annually to maintain world-class infrastructure.
3. Governance Budget (5%)
Funds enforcement, dispute resolution, standards development, and anti-corruption controls through the Fair Trade Committee. Even at moderate scale, this creates a $74.8 billion annual fund for protecting the system's integrity.

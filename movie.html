About I-RIS

Original stories. Independent vision. Built for global audiences.

Illy Robotic Instruments Studios (I-RIS) is the creative content arm of Illy Robotics, established to produce and distribute original entertainment across the most prominent film and streaming platforms in the world.

Our mission is to cultivate stories that resonate — from high-concept science fiction to raw, unscripted IRL content — and bring them to audiences on Tubi, Netflix, HBO, Network Television, and beyond.

What sets I-RIS apart:

  • A technology-first studio backed by the innovation pipeline of Illy Robotics
  • Original IP designed from day one for multi-platform distribution
  • A commitment to independent voices and underrepresented narratives
  • Bridging the gap between tech culture and mainstream entertainment

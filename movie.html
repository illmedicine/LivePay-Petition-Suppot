About I-RIS

Illy Robotic Instruments Studios (I-RIS) is the film production and content division of Illy Robotics — built to develop, produce, and distribute original entertainment to global audiences.

Like Zeus Network and Tubi, I-RIS cultivates original programming — from scripted sci-fi mini-series to raw IRL creator content — designed for the world's biggest streaming platforms and network television.

Our mission: bring independent, technology-driven stories to mainstream audiences and prove that original voices belong on the same stage as major studios.

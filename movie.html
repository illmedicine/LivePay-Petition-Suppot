The creator economy is fractured. Creators are forced to choose between exclusive subscription networks that offer revenue but lock content behind paywalls — costing up to 80% of their audience — and free social platforms that offer reach but no sustainable income. I-RIS is the bridge.

About I-RIS

Illy Robotic Instruments Studios (I-RIS) is the film production, content, and creator incubation division of Illy Robotics — built to develop, produce, and distribute original entertainment while empowering the creators who make it.

Unlike traditional networks that demand exclusivity, I-RIS operates on a non-exclusionary model. Creators keep their content everywhere — social media, streaming platforms, and live — while gaining access to studio-level production, a dedicated Creator's Corner dashboard, and a direct pipeline to major networks.

Our mission: bridge the gap between creators, networks, and fans — proving that original voices belong on the same stage as major studios, without sacrificing reach, revenue, or community.

I-RIS cultivates original programming — from scripted sci-fi mini-series to raw IRL creator content — designed for the world's biggest streaming platforms and network television. We are the first platform to function as both a creator incubator and a network scaler.
